## Research Summary

I am a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research focuses on multimodal generative models, embodied AI, and world modeling, with the goal of building intelligent agents that can understand, plan, and act in real-world environments. My work spans diffusion-based decision-making, autonomous driving planning, flow-matching-based behavior modeling, and 3D articulated object generation, with publications at ICLR and NeurIPS. Looking ahead, I aim to develop multimodal foundation models and generative world models that integrate vision, language, action, and 3D structure for practical AI agents that can perceive, simulate, and interact with the physical world.
